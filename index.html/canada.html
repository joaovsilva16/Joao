Hino do Canadal

            O Canada!
            Our home and native land
            True patriot love in all of us command
            
            With glowing hearts, we see thee rise
            The True North strong and free
            
            From far and wide
            O Canada, we stand on guard for thee
            
            God keep our land glorious and free
            O Canada, we stand on guard for thee
            O Canada, we stand on guard for thee
